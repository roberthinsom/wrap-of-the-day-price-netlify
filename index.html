What is McDonald’s Wrap of the Day?

McDonald’s Wrap of the Day is a rotating daily offering of wraps designed to provide a tasty meal that’s easy to enjoy during busy workdays, commutes, or after school. These wraps are typically made with high-quality ingredients such as crispy chicken, tender beef, fresh vegetables, and flavorful sauces, all wrapped in a soft tortilla for a convenient, hand-held meal. The wrap options change daily, giving customers the chance to enjoy something new every day of the week.
